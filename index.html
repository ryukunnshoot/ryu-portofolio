Tentang saya

Yo bro, gw Ryu, fotografer muda umur 16 tahun yang lagi semangat banget eksplorasi dunia lewat lensa kamera! Gw mulai dari kamera HP, pake lensa tambahan kayak Apexel, dan suka motret langit malam, makro dedaunan, dan momen sehari-hari yang punya cerita. Buat gw, fotografi itu bukan soal alat, tapi soal sudut pandang dan rasa. Gw juga sering upload hasil foto ke Instagram dan pengen terus berkembang jadi fotografer profesional ke depannya. Jangan ragu buat mampir ke galeri gw ya!

About Me

Yo! I'm Ryu, a 16-year-old passionate young photographer who loves to explore the world through a lens. I started with a phone camera, using extra lenses like Apexel, and I enjoy shooting the night sky, macro leaves, and everyday moments with a story. For me, photography isn't about the gear, it's about perspective and emotion. I frequently upload my work to Instagram and aim to grow into a professional photographer.
